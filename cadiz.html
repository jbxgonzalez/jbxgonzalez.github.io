Cádiz

I was lucky to be born on one of the oldest cities of Western Europe. Its unique history has created a city with unparalleled personality and architecture, attracting more and more tourists every year. Touristification is now pushing out many neighbors from their houses and neighborhoods, and many have left the city entirely. This collection of pictures is a tribute to the personality of my hometown, and a warning against the effects of mass tourism which extend beyond its walls. Cádiz for whom work it.
